FAQs

Ownership Questions

Ownership provides you with complete control of your vehicle, including customization options and no mileage restrictions.

Our comprehensive warranty covers all major components for 3 years or 36,000 miles, whichever comes first.

Yes, warranties are fully transferable to subsequent owners with minimal paperwork required.

We offer competitive financing through our network of lenders, with terms ranging from 24 to 84 months.

Vehicle Maintenance Questions

Oil changes every 7,500 miles, tire rotations every 10,000 miles, and comprehensive inspections annually.

Service is available at any authorized dealer nationwide, or at certified independent service centers.

Full service history is available through your online owner's portal or at any authorized dealership.

Many basic maintenance items like checking fluids, changing wiper blades, and replacing air filters can be done at home.

Technology Questions

Our system features intuitive touch controls, voice commands, and smartphone integration with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto.

Updates are released quarterly and can be installed automatically via Wi-Fi or at your dealership.

Standard safety features include adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, automatic emergency braking, and blind spot monitoring.

Yes, remote start is available through our smartphone app in all trims above the base model.

Electric Vehicle Questions

Our batteries are designed to retain at least 80% capacity after 8 years or 100,000 miles.

You can charge at home with our included Level 2 charger, or at any public charging station nationwide.

Fast charging can get you to 80% in under 30 minutes, while home charging typically takes 8-10 hours for a full charge.

EVs require minimal maintenance compared to gas vehicles - mainly tire rotations, brake inspections, and cabin air filter changes.

Towing Questions

Depending on model and configuration, our vehicles can tow between 5,000 and 12,000 pounds when properly equipped.

We recommend factory-installed hitch receivers with integrated wiring harnesses for optimal performance and safety.

Warranty coverage remains intact when operating within published towing limits with manufacturer-approved equipment.

When towing, increase following distance, anticipate braking earlier, use lower gears in hilly terrain, and distribute load evenly.
